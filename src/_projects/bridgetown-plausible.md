## Usage

Install the gem, initialize the plugin in Bridgetown, configure your Plausible domain, and render the helper in your document head.

## Backstory

Plausible is a lightweight, privacy-friendly analytics option. This plugin removes the repeated setup work of adding the tracking script to Bridgetown sites.
